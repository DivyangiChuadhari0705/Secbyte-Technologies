About Us

Welcome to MyShop! We're an online demo eCommerce store created to help learners practice frontend development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Our mission is to provide a realistic online store layout without any backend or real payment processing, giving you the freedom to experiment and build your frontend skills.

Feel free to browse products, add items to the cart, and explore our simple site design. All data is stored locally in your browser (using localStorage).
